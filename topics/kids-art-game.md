# Kids Art Game

## Overview

Creative art/drawing game for young children on iPad.

## Target

- **Age:** 5-8 years old
- **Platform:** iPad (touch-first, Apple Pencil optional)
- **Category:** Kids / Education / Creativity

## Core Concept

An art/creativity app that makes drawing and creating fun and accessible for young kids.

## Ideas to Explore

### Drawing Mechanics
- Finger-friendly brushes and tools
- Apple Pencil support for older kids
- Simple color palettes (not overwhelming)
- Undo/redo with kid-friendly UI
- Stickers and stamps

### Guided Activities
- Step-by-step drawing tutorials (draw a cat, a house, etc.)
- Coloring pages with outlines
- Connect-the-dots that reveal pictures
- Tracing exercises

### Creative Freedom
- Blank canvas mode
- Photo backgrounds to draw on
- Animated drawings (simple frame-by-frame)
- Add sounds to drawings

### Gamification
- Daily drawing challenges
- Collectible brushes/colors unlocked by drawing
- "Gallery" to save and view creations
- Achievements for completing tutorials

### Social (Safe)
- Share with parents only (no public sharing)
- Family gallery (shared with family members)
- Print drawings

### Monetization Ideas
- Free with limited brushes/colors
- One-time purchase to unlock everything
- NO ads (kid-safe)
- NO subscriptions (parents hate them for kids apps)

## Inspiration / Competition

- Tayasui Sketches
- Procreate (but way simpler)
- Drawing for Kids (various apps)
- Toca Boca style (playful, no pressure)
